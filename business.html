Luxury Made Easy; Plan Less, Experience More

The name Tripsandgo captures the idea of easy, spontaneous travel; “plan your trips and go!” It represents speed,comfort and convenience for travelers who want hassle-free experiences.

Tripsandgo is a modern travel and tour agency that helps individuals and groups discover the best destinations; both local and international,with comfort, luxury, and affordability. We provide end-to-end travel services, from trip planning and visa assistance to hotel booking and tours, ensuring every journey is smooth, stylish, and unforgettable.

Flight Booking

We offer both domestic and international flight reservations at competitive rates, compare airlines and get the best deals, last-minute and flexible ticket options available and support for group bookings and business travel.

Hotel Reservations

Stay anywhere in the world with ease; Curated list of hotels, resorts, and apartments to fit any budget, Partnered with verified properties for safety and quality & also option for special requests.

Tour Packages

Custom vacation packages that match your budget and interests; Honeymoon, solo adventures, family holidays,corporate retreats and exciting destinations across Africa, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Visa Assistance

We simplify your visa process for stress-free travel, guild you on visa requirements for any destination. For Student, business, tourist and family visa categories.

Travel Insurance

Protect your trip from the unexpected, coverage for medical emergencies, delays and lost of baggage & flexible plans for short or long stays.

Study-Abroad Trips

Helping students explore global opportunities, educational tours and exchange programs for schools. We assistance with admission processes, visa and travel.

About Us

Welcome to Tripsandgo, where your dream journey begins.

At Tripsandgo, we believe travel should be more than just moving from one place to another; it should be a story worth telling. We specialize in creating luxurious, seamless, and unforgettable travel experiences for individuals, couples, families, and groups who love to explore the world in style.

Whether it's a romantic getaway to Santorini, a business trip to Dubai, or a cultural escape across Africa, our team is dedicated to handling every detail; from flights and hotel bookings to guided tours and personalized itineraries. With trusted global partners and a passion for excellence, we ensure that every trip is smooth, safe, and filled with unforgettable moments.

Our Mission

At Tripsandgo, the mission is to deliver tailor-made travel experiences by understanding clients' preferences, offering curated itineraries, and providing exceptional service. The overall aim is to simplify travel planning while fostering a deeper connection between people and their destinations.

Our Vision

Tripsandgo's vision is to be the leading travel platform that transforms journeys into uniquely personalized experiences, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Story

Our story began with Eji, who had the vision of creating a traveling company since Jeremiah had already helped tons of people throughout the years. Jeremiah, already assisting numerous people in Africa and the United Kingdom with visa applications, officially established the company in Tallinn, Estonia in 2023. Ever since our teams grew person by person to help more and more people. Today, we are a group of five dedicated individuals helping hundreds of people worldwide achieve their travel goals without a worry!
